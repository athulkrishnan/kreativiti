About Us

Kreativiti Images is a group of visual artists based out of Chennai, India. We were students at IIT Madras
when our shared passion for photography brought us together to form Kreativiti. Yassin and Pratheesh have an
engineering background, while Athul and Sajith come from literature and filmmaking backgrounds respectively.
The diversity of our backgrounds and experiences make us a truly distinctive team.

We are a client-friendly, service-oriented agency and believe that great creativity often is the result of team
effort, philosophy and values. Our team is ready to create outstanding visuals for you, and make sure that you
can cherish your precious moments for eternity. We believe in the decisive moment as proposed by Henri Cartier-Bresson ,
and we strive to freeze moments that stand the test of time.
